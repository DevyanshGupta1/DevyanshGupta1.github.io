Rivanta Resort Cancellation Policy

At Rivanta Resorts, we understand that plans may change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair while respecting the effort involved in reserving your stay.
Description

1- Free cancellation Policy
Guests receive a full refund for cancellations made up to 3 days before arrival. Cancellations within 3 days of arrival or no-shows will be charged the full reservation cost. If the hotel cannot accommodate the confirmed reservation, the Hotel can help by providing an alternate and similar accommodation and is not liable to pay any compensation/damages.

2-Festive Cancellation Policy
Guests receive a 50% refund for cancellations made up to 15 days before arrival. Cancellations between 15 to 7 days of arrival guest will be charged 75% of the booking amount. Cancellation within 7 days of arrival or no-shows will be charged the full reservation cost. If the hotel cannot accommodate the confirmed reservation, the Hotel can help by providing an alternate and similar accommodation and is not liable to pay any compensation/damages.
