About Rivanta Resorts

Welcome to Rivanta Resorts — a serene escape in the heart of Kasol. Our resort offers luxurious accommodations, breathtaking views, and personalized experiences that make your stay unforgettable.

Explore our amenities, indulge in local cuisine, or embark on adventures curated by our expert team. We’re here to craft your story with comfort and elegance.

Privacy Policy

At Rivanta Resorts, your privacy is important to us. We ensure that all personal information shared with us is handled securely and responsibly.

Information collected during bookings, inquiries, or reviews is used only to enhance guest experience and for operational purposes. We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.

By using our services, you consent to our privacy practices as outlined above.

Cancellation Policy

Reservations canceled up to 7 days prior to the check-in date are eligible for a full refund.

Cancellations made within 7 days of check-in may incur charges depending on the booking terms.

No-shows or early check-outs are non-refundable unless otherwise stated during booking.
